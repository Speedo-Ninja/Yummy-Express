Help & Support

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the 'Order History' section.

What should I do if my order is delayed?

If your order is delayed, please contact our support team immediately for assistance.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can only be modified or canceled within 10 minutes of placing the order. Contact support for more details.

How do I redeem a discount code?

During checkout, you can enter your discount code in the 'Promo Code' section to apply the discount.
