Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I place an order?

To place an order, browse our menu, select your favorite dishes, and add them to your cart. Once you’re ready, proceed to checkout and enter your delivery details.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We mostly prefer UPI Transaction at the time of Food Delivery. You can also pay cash on delivery depending on your location.

3. How can I track my order?

You can track your order status directly through your account or by clicking the 'Track Order' button after checkout.

4. Can I change my order after it's placed?

Once an order is placed, it cannot be modified. However, you can cancel it within 15 minutes and place a new one , But remember doing multiple Cancelation order can harm your account Credit of Response.
