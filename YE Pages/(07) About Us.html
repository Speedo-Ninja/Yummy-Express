About Yummy Express

Yummy Express is an emerging online food delivery platform founded in May 2023 by two passionate individuals — Parnay Yadav and Rohan Kumar (techinical developer). The company was born out of a shared vision to revolutionize food delivery in Koderma District, Jharkhand, by making fast, reliable, and delicious meals accessible to everyone at their doorstep. Our journey began with a simple idea: to provide quick and hassle-free food delivery to the citizens of Koderma. Initially, we started taking orders through WhatsApp Business, catering to customers across the district. As technology evolved and demand grew, we took a major step forward by launching the Yummy Express website, offering a smoother, more user-friendly experience for our customers. Today, Yummy Express stands as a trusted name in the region, known for our commitment to quality, speed, and customer satisfaction. We continue to grow with the same passion and dedication that fueled our beginnings, always striving to serve you better.
